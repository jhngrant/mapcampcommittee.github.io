Map Camp London 2019, 15th October

Tickets

Sponsorship Opportunities

This is our third Map Camp in London and it is kindly sponsored by the Leading Leading Edge Forum, Rainmaker, AWS, Methods, Superstars and InfoQ. Map Camp is a community run event, it consists of a series of lectures from renowned speakers with a coffee and networking area to meet other mappers from across the globe. The original event in 2017 was planned to be 30 people, we ended up with 180 and over 150 on a waiting list. In 2018, we planned for 350 people, we ended up with 450 and over 260 on a waiting list. This year, we have an even bigger venue in the hope that we might be able to accommodate everyone.

Map Camp is a safe environment for all and we will do everything we can to make sure that it is. If you're planning on attending please read the code of conduct.
